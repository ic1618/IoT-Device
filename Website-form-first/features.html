    Injury prevention


    The number one reason keeping people out of the gym is an injury. The top three occurring injuries in the gym are the lower back, the shoulders and the knees. All of which can be contributed to improper form when doing the most likely causing exercises: squats, deadlifts and over-head press. Strategically located flex sensors will be able to record and relay any points throughout a set where your technique was slacking. Injury prevention would be the most important thing a personal trainer would be focused on. 

    Calorie burn rate


    Several studies also show there is a clear optimal temperature to exercises at both for safety concerns and maximum calorie burn. The essential body temperature for training is around 37.5 degrees Celsius. Muscles and ligaments that aren’t warmed up properly are a greater risk of tearing and a higher calorie burn is more beneficial to fat loss. Thermopile sensors can record your body temperature throughout a workout and remind you when you have entered that sweet spot temperature.
