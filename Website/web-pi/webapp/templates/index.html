    Features

    Integratable

    Form first is compatible with multiple joints that are prone to injury, including for example the wrist, shoulders, lower back and knees. The device is also integratable with commonly used gym equipment such as wrist straps, lifting belts, and knee sleeves.

    Lightweight

    Along with being long lasting and durable, the Form First device is extremely lightweight and doesn’t interfere with your workouts.

    Easy to user interface

    Communication between the device and website/application is fast and reliable. It is capable of providing immediate and accurate feedback.

    Fast and wireless connection

    User friendly and ergonomically designed interface with easy to access data on your workouts. Data is processed, formatted and clearly presented to the customer.

    Competition


    Unlike Form First, there are dozens of fitness applications available all offer the same service: personalised workouts and food plans. Gym maniacs, exercise enthusiasts and new goers are bombarded with the same overload of information. Form First avoids this heavily saturated market area of the fitness industry. It instead targets a gaping whole in the market with very few competitors. 

    Demand Reasoning

    The demand for a product like this has shot up since the beginning of the pandemic due to the closure of gyms. Vast increasing numbers of the public have been buying up their own gym equipment and are working out at home. The proof of such dramatic increase of home workouts is clear from the price spike in gym equipment of over 5,800% since March 2020.1 Training at home, however, comes with its downsides. Unfortunately, from a lack of hand on guidance from licensed personal trainers, inexperienced weight lifters will begin to develop many injuries from incorrect technique during these home workouts.
